# Reply Ladder Template

> **Use:** fill this into Section 5 (Engagement playbook) of the playbook. The "ladder" is a tiered approach to whose content the founder engages with, and how.

## Tier 1 — Daily / every-other-day engagement (5–10 handles)

These are the accounts whose posts the founder should reply to consistently. Reply should **add substance** — not "great point!" — but a data point, a counter, a related example, or a sharpening.

| Handle | Why this account | Reply mode |
|---|---|---|
| @{{HANDLE_1}} | {{ONE_LINE_REASON_1}} | {{SUBSTANTIVE_ADD / COUNTER / RELATED_EXAMPLE}} |
| @{{HANDLE_2}} | {{ONE_LINE_REASON_2}} | {{REPLY_MODE_2}} |
| @{{HANDLE_3}} | {{ONE_LINE_REASON_3}} | {{REPLY_MODE_3}} |
| @{{HANDLE_4}} | {{ONE_LINE_REASON_4}} | {{REPLY_MODE_4}} |
| @{{HANDLE_5}} | {{ONE_LINE_REASON_5}} | {{REPLY_MODE_5}} |

**Time budget:** 15–20 minutes per day.

## Tier 2 — Weekly engagement (15–25 handles)

These are accounts worth checking in with once a week — read their recent posts, reply to the one or two that land for the founder's voice. Lower-frequency but still intentional.

| Handle | Why this account |
|---|---|
| @{{T2_HANDLE_1}} | {{T2_REASON_1}} |
| @{{T2_HANDLE_2}} | {{T2_REASON_2}} |
| @{{T2_HANDLE_3}} | {{T2_REASON_3}} |
| ... | ... |

**Time budget:** 30 minutes per week (one sitting).

## Reply templates by mode

Templates are scaffolds, not scripts. Adapt to the specific post.

### Substantive add
> Adds to this: [specific data point or related example]. [Optional — the implication].
>
> Length: 1–3 sentences. Precise.

### Friendly counter
> Respectfully disagree on [specific point]. My read: [specific counter, with reasoning]. Happy to be wrong — what's your take?
>
> Length: 1–3 sentences. Specific, not generic.

### Related example
> Reminds me of [adjacent example or historical analog]. The parallel: [one-sentence connection].
>
> Length: 1–2 sentences.

### Sharpening
> One sharpening: [restate the poster's idea in a tighter form, add what they implied but didn't say].
>
> Length: 1 sentence. Use sparingly — only when you genuinely see something sharper.

### Question (low-frequency)
> Curious how you think about [specific aspect they didn't cover]? Asking because [brief reason].
>
> Length: 1–2 sentences. Use only when the question is genuine and specific — generic questions read as engagement-farming.

## What not to do (applies to all tiers)

- Don't reply "🔥" or "this" or "great take!" — adds nothing, reads as reputation-harvesting.
- Don't reply to every post by a Tier 1 handle. Be selective.
- Don't argue publicly with other Solana founders unless it's a substantive technical or ecosystem disagreement. In-ecosystem dunking compounds badly.
- Don't reply to accounts >1000x your follower count unless you have something specifically additive. Usually better to quote-tweet with your own take.
- Don't copy-paste template wording across replies. Adapt every time.

## Spaces and AMA ladder

Beyond text replies, engagement includes:

- **Showing up in Tier 1 Spaces** — audience presence, occasional request-to-speak when genuinely additive.
- **Guesting on an ecosystem podcast/Space** 1–2× per month when you have something specific to say.
- **Hosting your own Space** — only once you've built some presence. Start as a co-host or guest first.
